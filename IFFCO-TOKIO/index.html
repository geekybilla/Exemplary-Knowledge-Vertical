INSURANCE

Insurance is a contract, represented by a policy, in which a policyholder receives financial protection or reimbursement against losses from an insurance company. The company pools client's risks to make payments more affordable for the insured. Most people have some insurance: for their car, their house, their healthcare, or their life. Insurance policies hedge against financial losses resulting from accidents, injury, or property damage. Insurance also helps cover costs associated with liability (legal responsibility) for damage or injury caused to a third party.

Health Insurance

What is Health Insurance? A Health insurance policy also called as medical insurance offers coverage against a range of medical, surgical, prescription drug, and dental expenses you may face when you fall sick or meet with an accident. A good health insurance policy covers hospital expenses, pre-and post-hospitalization costs, daycare treatments, laboratory services, prescription drugs, ambulance charges & daily allowance benefit. The claim for your medical insurance policy can be availed cashless or with the help of bills that you have paid to cover the expenses.

Motor Insurance


Motor insurance or vehicle insurance is a type of insurance cover specifically designed for four wheelers, cars, two wheelers, commercial vehicles, scooters, bike, tractors, trucks etc. A motor insurance protects the insured vehicle from financial losses due to accident or any other natural calamities. A motor insurance also provides protection against third party liabilities.

Home Insurance


Home insurance is a type of insurance that protect your home along with its furnishing and other valuable items from any kind of man-made or natural calamities. Generally, it covers home exterior damage, interior damage, damage to furnishing or injuries to person that arises while on the insured home.
