HEALTH INSURANCE

What is Health Insurance?

A Health insurance policy also called as medical insurance offers coverage against a range of medical, surgical, prescription drug, and dental expenses you may face when you fall sick or meet with an accident. A good health insurance policy covers hospital expenses, pre-and post-hospitalization costs, daycare treatments, laboratory services, prescription drugs, ambulance charges & daily allowance benefit. You also get your expenses covered for Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy treatments. The claim for your medical insurance policy can be availed cashless or with the help of bills that you have paid to cover the expenses. A health insurance policy helps you to stay covered against various illnesses and injuries. Additionally it helps you save taxes upto ₹75,000 under section 80D of Income Tax Act, 1961.

Why is Health Insurance important?

The reasons why health insurance is important for you and your family includes:
  • Health insurance protects your saving in case of medical emergency or illness
  • Helps you in affording quality healthcare despite high cost of treatment
  • Save Tax upto ₹1,00,000 under section 80D
  • Provide coverage against Lifestyle diseases like diabetes, heart disease etc.
  • Provide extensive coverage against rising medical costs
  • Provide access to Specialized Treatment
  • Provide access to Cashless Treatment
