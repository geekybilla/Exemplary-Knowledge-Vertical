MOTOR INSURANCE

What is a Motor Insurance?

Motor insurance or vehicle insurance is a type of insurance cover specifically designed for four wheelers, cars, two wheelers, commercial vehicles, scooters, bike, tractors, trucks etc. A motor insurance protects the insured vehicle from financial losses due to accident or any other natural calamities. A motor insurance also provides protection against third party liabilities.

Why do you need Motor Insurance in India?

You require Motor insurance in India due to following reasons –
1. Mandated by Law - Having a Motor insurance plan is compulsory as per the Motor Vehicles Act.
2. Covers Repair Costs - A Motor insurance policy ensures that your vehicle is restored to its original condition without putting a dent in your finances.
3. Covers Third-Party Liabilities - Motor insurance plan will cover your legal liability for third party losses.
